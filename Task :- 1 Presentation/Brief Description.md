Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

I created my infographic using Canva, a simple and user-friendly design tool. My design explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. It includes topics such as useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

I used clear headings, short points, and icons to make the information easy to understand and visually appealing. The infographic helps students learn how to use technology safely, communicate properly, and build a positive online identity.

One challenge I faced was arranging the content neatly without making the design look crowded. However, I enjoyed choosing colors and icons to make the infographic more attractive. This project improved my creativity and helped me better understand digital literacy.
